You enjoy being alone and wish to remain so... This site is also for you!

You will be able to use this advanced search to find, just like the basic search, people who have already been to the town/country that you have picked as your next destination. Furthermore, thanks to all of the useful criteria, you will be able to find people with the same centers of interest.

Will the latter be sufficient and pertinent enough to enable you to hit it off with people and discuss your travel plans to the full?... You alone have the answer!

You will no doubt be able to obtain helpful information through other people’s experiences and the useful information they share via this new platform, and why not make the most of some of their recommendations.

In order to do so you can use this search by entering dates in the past and then chatting with people to your heart’s content in order to prepare your next trip/day out.

You will then also be able to use the search by entering dates in the future if:

  • You change your mind at the last minute.
  • You need help once you’ve arrived at your chosen destination.
  • You haven’t found anyone who has visited the place in question.
  • You want to help a friend who hasn't had time to sign up to the site and who wants to find a travel companion.

Who better than fellow travellers to help you: travel like they do, make the most of their feedback and share practical information about the countries they have visited?
